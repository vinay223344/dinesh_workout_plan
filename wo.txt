🏠 FINAL WEEKLY PLAN (12 WEEKS)
📅 Weekly Structure
🟦 Mon – Push (Chest + Shoulders + Triceps)
🟨 Tue – Run (3 km) + Core
🟩 Wed – Pull (Back + Biceps)
🟥 Thu – Legs + Knock-knee correction
🟦 Fri – Run (3 km) + Fat burn
⚪ Sat – FASTING + FULL REST
🟪 Sun – Full Body (light shape + pump)
🟦 MON – PUSH (Chest focus)
Floor barbell press – 4 × 8–12
Push-ups – 4 sets (max reps, slow)
Dumbbell shoulder press – 3 × 10
Lateral raises – 3 × 12–15
Chair dips – 3 × 10–15

👉 Slow reps = better chest tightening

🟨 TUE – RUN + CORE
3 km run (steady pace)
Plank – 3 × 45–60 sec
Leg raises – 3 × 12
Mountain climbers – 3 × 30 sec
Bicycle crunch – 3 × 15 each side
🟩 WED – PULL (Back + Biceps)
Barbell bent-over row – 4 × 8–12
One-arm dumbbell row – 3 × 10 each side
Dumbbell curls – 3 × 10–12
Hammer curls – 3 × 10
Rear delt fly – 3 × 12–15
🟥 THU – LEGS + KNOCK KNEE FIX
Squats (barbell or goblet) – 4 × 10–12
Reverse lunges – 3 × 10 each leg
Glute bridge – 4 × 12–15
Calf raises – 4 × 15–20
Wall sit – 2 × 60 sec

👉 Knee rule: always push knees slightly outward

🟦 FRI – RUN + FAT BURN
3 km run (slightly faster than Tuesday)
Jumping jacks – 3 × 40 sec
Burpees – 3 × 8–12
High knees – 3 × 30 sec
Plank – 3 × 1 min
⚪ SAT – FASTING + FULL REST
No workout
Only light walking if needed
Optional stretching (5–10 min)

👉 This is your recovery + fat-loss reset day

🟪 SUN – FULL BODY (LIGHT SHAPE DAY)
Push-ups – 3 sets
Squats – 3 sets
Barbell rows – 3 sets
Shoulder press – 3 sets
Biceps curls – 3 sets
Glute bridge – 3 sets
Plank – 2 sets

👉 Keep it moderate (don’t exhaust yourself)

🔥 Calories: 1800–2000 kcal

🌿 Fiber: high (your current diet is fine)
